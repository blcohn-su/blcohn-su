Writing Samples


Writing Sample #1: Public Relations' Role in the Pharmaceutical Industry

I detail the major role public relations plays in the pharmaceutical industry. This improves the functions of organizations by placing individuals' needs as their main priority and increases their credibility.


Writing Sample #2: Twentieth Century Fox Consumer Products and the Role of Public Relations

In this paper, I interviewed a member of the Consumer Products division of Twentieth Century Fox. I gained an in-depth understanding of the role public relations plays in this department.

Writing Sample #3: Chrissy Teigen's Presence in the Media

Chrissy Teigen is a well-known celebrity, model and reality show star. In this paper, I delve into the way she portrays herself in the media. To do so, I compare and contrast her usage of two different platforms.

Writing Sample #4: Toxic Masculinity in American Society

Toxic masculinity is a result of socially defined gender roles which have caused men to believe they must act a certain way to fulfill their role. In this essay, I discuss the detrimental effects this social construct has, not only on males, but on society as a whole.

Writing Sample #5: The Impact of Disney Princesses on Children

Disney is a household name for individuals all over the world. However, this iconic corporation is more than just a fantasy, the prevalence of Disney films perpetuates gender stereotypes in children.

Writing Sample #6: Syracuse University Increases Safety in Residence Halls

Syracuse University increased its security presence in all on-campus residence halls beginning in the spring 2020 semester. Security personnel will begin monitoring at all hours of the day.
